Where I’ve learned by building

Design Engineer · Crew Tool

Sep 2024 – Sep 2025

Crew Tool is a precision tooling startup supporting aerospace manufacturing. As Design Engineer, I focused on mechanical design, ergonomic tooling, and high-reliability drawing practices.

  • Engineered precise pneumatic systems to regulate sealant gun pressure, balancing safety and performance under tight tolerances.
  • Ran in-depth quality inspections using FT-IR, Hexane testing, and dimensional checks to validate tools against spec sheets.
  • Built purpose-driven tools for aerospace sealant application and removal, shaped by user feedback and field use cases.
  • Redesigned legacy tools to improve ergonomics and ease-of-use for operators without sacrificing performance.

Lead Engineer · Airgroup Dynamics Inc.

May 2021 – Sep 2024

Airgroup Dynamics provides repair, overhaul, and tooling services for aerospace clients. As Lead Engineer, I oversaw CAD, prototyping, and documentation of custom tools used in high-precision jet component repair workflows.

  • Designed custom tools that sped up aircraft maintenance workflows, cutting task time by 20% in high-throughput environments.
  • Produced detailed technical drawings under ASME Y14.5 standards and developed in-house drafting conventions.
  • Set up a small-scale additive manufacturing lab to rapidly prototype concepts and troubleshoot tooling issues on the fly.
  • Improved internal diagnostics and documentation for FAA compliance, from hands-on QA to capability audits.

Engineer Intern · Florida Space institute

Jan 2021 – May 2021

FSI is a research center focused on advancing space technologies in Florida. During my internship, I contributed to student outreach and supported program development focused on technical preparation and retention.

  • Helped simulate lunar surface conditions by designing and fabricating regolith experiment components used in cryo and microgravity tests.
  • Contributed to NASA-aligned research exploring how regolith reacts to impacts and collisions under space-like constraints.
  • Developed procedures for assembling and testing experimental hardware used in live lab environments.
  • Worked alongside faculty and engineers to merge academic research with hands-on engineering problem-solving.
